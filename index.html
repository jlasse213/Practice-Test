Question 4
Does the point (2, 9) exist on the line y = 3x + 4?
Solution:
Plug x = 2 into y = 3x + 4: y = 3(2) + 4 = 10. So (2, 9) is not on the line.
Question 5
What is the slope of the line between the points (6, 9) and (-7, 8)?
Solution:
The slope is 1/13 or approximately 0.0769 (since (9-8)/(6-(-7)) = 1/13).
